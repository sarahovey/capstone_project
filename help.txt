~Helper~
-inventory: get a look at your inventory
-take: take an item
-use: use an item in your inventory
-look [item]: closely inspect an item
-look around: get a desription of what you can see
-open [door]: open a door or gate to get to a different room. You may need to have the right key in your inventory!
-quit: quit the game

Other hints:
-Try talking to other characters when you find them!
-Try to interact with different items in the room. Think about how you might use them
